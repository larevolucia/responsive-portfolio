My experiences

Publishing & Content

Being a Literature graduate with a love for technology, is no wonder that I ended-up working on digital publishing for so many years. Even after leaving the publishing market, I continued to dive into new content related business, from mobile gaming to video-on-demand.

Here is a little taste of my experience:

  • Digital publishing management at Zahar and Rocco publishing houses in Brazil.
  • A jack of all trades at e-book and audiobook subscription start-up, Bookchoice
  • Co-founder and columnist of Colofão, a hands-on forum for the brazilian digital publishing industry.
  • Columnist and Social media coordenator at Revista Capitolina
  • Editor and product consultant for the choose your own-narrative game Story Beats, at Spil Games.
